My Areas of Interest

These are the areas in Computer Science which interest me the most.

Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the ability displayed by a machine in handling day-to-day tasks as opposed to natural intelligence displayed by humans and animals. In many ways AI will pave the path and direction for the future of computing.

Machine Learning

Machine Learning is the ability of a computer system to learn from past experiences. More often than not, it is without any structural data form. ML would make the computers of future samrter than ever, possibly more than humans.

Quantum Computing

As opposed to a classical computer, a quantum computer uses quantum mechanical concepts to produce output for any given input. It is blazingly fast. The field is still in its infancy and is majorly theoretical as of now.

Internet of Things

The Internet of Things is the interconnection of various devices over the Internet. The connected devices can be controlled using a central device or system. Among other things, IoT is the future of computing and its happening now.
